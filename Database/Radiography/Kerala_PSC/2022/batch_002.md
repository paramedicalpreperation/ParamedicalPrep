Shoulder joint is formed by —————— of scapula:
Option A:
Glenoid
Option B:
Coracoid

Acromion
Option D:
Spinous process
Correct Answer:
A
Difficulty:
Easy
Explanation:
The glenohumeral (shoulder) joint is a highly mobile ball-and-socket joint formed by the articulation of the head of the humerus with the shallow glenoid cavity (or fossa) of the scapula.
