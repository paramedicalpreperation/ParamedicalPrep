## Q23
Question:
What is the main mode of heat transfer in rotating anode X-ray tube?
Option A:
Conduction
Option B:
Convention
Option C:
Radiation
Option D:
All the above
Correct Answer:
C
Difficulty:
Medium
Explanation:
In a rotating anode X-ray tube, the anode is located in a vacuum. Therefore, heat cannot be dissipated through conduction (which requires a physical medium) or convection (which requires gas or liquid). The primary method for removing heat from the glowing hot anode disc is thermal radiation (infrared emission).
Source:
Kerala PSC X-ray Technician 2016
