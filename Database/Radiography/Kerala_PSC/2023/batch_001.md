'The body of a person who gets an electric shock is to be massaged well'. Reason for this statement is based on which property of blood?
Option A:
Capillarity
Option B:
Density
Option C:
Elasticity
Option D:
Viscosity
Correct Answer:
D

Medium

## Q4
Question:
A capacitor is used in the primary circuit of an induction coil
Option A:
To block dc and allow ac
Option B:
To supply voltage to the circuit
Option C:
To avoid sparking by the high induced voltage
Option D:
To eliminate electric ripples
Correct Answer:
C
Difficulty:
Medium
Explanation:
In an induction coil, breaking the primary current induces a very high voltage. Without a capacitor (condenser), this surge would cause a large spark at the contact breaker (interrupter). The capacitor absorbs this sudden energy surge, preventing sparking and ensuring a faster collapse of the magnetic field, which increases the induced secondary voltage.

1.53 A
Option B:
15.3 A
Option C:
0.153 A
Option D:

Correct Answer:

Hard
Explanation:
Using the shunt formula: Ig * G = Is * S, where Ig is galvanometer current (0.03 A), G is resistance (500 Ω), and S is shunt resistance (10 Ω). Is = (0.03 * 500) / 10 = 1.5 A. Total current I = Ig + Is = 0.03 + 1.5 = 1.53 A.
