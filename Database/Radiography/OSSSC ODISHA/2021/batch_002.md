## Q34

## Q49
Question:
What is the oxygen effect?
Option A:
Detrimental effect of oxygen in presence of therapeutic radiations
Option B:
A positive effect of oxygen in absence of therapeutic radiations
Option C:
Enhancement effect of oxygen in presence of therapeutic radiation
Option D:
Presence of oxygen during radiation treatment
Correct Answer:
Option C
Difficulty:
Medium
Explanation:
The "oxygen effect" refers to the increased sensitivity of cells to ionizing radiation in the presence of oxygen, which acts as a radiation sensitizer.
