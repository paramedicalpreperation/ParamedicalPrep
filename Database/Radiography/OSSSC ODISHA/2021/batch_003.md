## Q84
Question:
The wave velocity of ultrasound in soft tissues is 1540 m/s and the impedance offered by it is 1.63 \times 10^6 \text{ kg/m}^2\text{s}. What is the density of the soft tissue?
Option A:
0.1058 \text{ kg/m}^3
Option B:
10.58 \text{ kg/m}^3
Option C:
1058.4 \text{ kg/m}^3
Option D:
105844.1 \text{ kg/m}^3
Correct Answer:
Option C
Difficulty:
Hard
Explanation:
Using the formula Z = \rho \cdot v (where Z is impedance, \rho is density, and v is velocity), \rho = Z / v = 1.63 \times 10^6 / 1540 \approx 1058.4 \text{ kg/m}^3.
