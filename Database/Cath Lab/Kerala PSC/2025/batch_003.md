## Q51
Question:
Which is the S.I. unit for effective dose of radiation?
Option A:
Gray (Gy)
Option B:
Sievert (Sv)
Option C:
Roentgen (R)
Option D:
RAD
Correct Answer:
B
Difficulty:
Easy
Explanation:
The Sievert (Sv) is the SI unit for the effective dose of ionizing radiation, accounting for the biological effects on different tissues. The Gray (Gy) measures the absorbed dose.
