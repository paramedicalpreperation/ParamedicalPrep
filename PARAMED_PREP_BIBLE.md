# PARAMED PREP BIBLE

## Vision

Paramed Prep is a complete learning platform for Paramedical students.

It is not just an MCQ application.

Every design decision must prioritize learning, simplicity, scalability and user experience.

---

## Core Principles

Learning First

Never disturb students while learning.

Premium should unlock better learning, not basic learning.

Advertisements should never interrupt important study sessions.
